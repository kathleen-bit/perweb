Why Paris?

Paris, the "City of Light," is a global center of art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral make it a must-visit destination for travelers.


How to Get There

By Air: Paris is served by Charles de Gaulle (CDG) and Orly (ORY) airports.

By Train: Eurostar connects Paris to London and Brussels via Gare du Nord.

Public Transport: Metro, buses, and trams provide easy access around the city.


Other Places to Visit

Louvre Museum: Home to thousands of works, including the Mona Lisa.

Champs-Élysées: Famous avenue leading to the Arc de Triomphe.

Notre-Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.

Montmartre: Bohemian district with stunning city views.
