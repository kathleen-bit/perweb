Why Athens?

Athens, the cradle of Western civilization, offers a mesmerizing blend of ancient history and vibrant modern culture. It's home to iconic landmarks like the Acropolis, Parthenon, and the charming Plaka neighborhood.


How to Get There

By Air: Fly to Athens International Airport (ATH), which serves numerous international flights.

By Train: Trains connect Athens with Thessaloniki and other Greek cities.

Public Transport: The Athens Metro and buses offer easy access around the city.
