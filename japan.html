How to Get There

By Air: Fly to Kansai International Airport (KIX) and take a train or bus to Kyoto.

By Train: The Shinkansen bullet train connects Kyoto to Tokyo and Osaka.

Public Transport: Kyoto's bus and subway system offers easy access to popular tourist sites.


Other Places to Visit

Fushimi Inari Shrine: Famous for its thousands of vermilion torii gates.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove: A serene bamboo forest with enchanting beauty.

Kiyomizu-dera: A UNESCO-listed Buddhist temple with breathtaking views.

Gion District: A historic area where you can spot geishas.
