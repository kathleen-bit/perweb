Why Wengen & Interlaken?

Wengen is a charming car-free village surrounded by the Swiss Alps, offering breathtaking views, cozy chalets, and hiking trails. Interlaken, nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz, is famous for its adventure sports and scenic boat rides.


How to Get There

By Air: Fly to Zurich or Geneva, then take a scenic train to Interlaken.

By Train: From Interlaken, take a direct train to Wengen through Lauterbrunnen.


Other Places to Visit

Jungfraujoch: The “Top of Europe,” with panoramic views.

Grindelwald: Perfect for hiking and exploring the Bernese Alps.

Lake Thun & Lake Brienz: Enjoy boat cruises with stunning mountain views.
