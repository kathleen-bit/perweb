Hello and thanks for stopping by! This is where I share my all-time favorite TV shows and movies that have left a lasting impression on me. TV Shows

Here are some of my favorite TV shows!

Modern Family

A hilarious mockumentary that follows the lives of the Pritchett-Dunphy-Tucker family, offering a funny and heartfelt look at modern-day family dynamics.

Main Leads: Ed O'Neill, Sofia Vergara, Julie Bowen, Ty Burrell | Year: 2009 | Genre: Comedy, Family, Romance

Running Man (Korea)

A South Korean variety show where contestants complete missions in a race to win, packed with humor, action, and surprising twists.

Main Leads: Yoo Jae-suk, Kim Jong-kook, Ha Dong-hoon, Song Ji-hyo | Year: 2010 | Genre: Variety, Comedy, Reality

Movies

Here are some of my favorite movies!

Case Closed: The Phantom of Baker Street

Conan is transported to a virtual reality game set in 19th-century London where he must solve a mystery to save the lives of the players.

Main Leads: Minami Takayama, Wakana Yamazaki | Year: 2002 | Genre: Animation, Mystery, Adventure

Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle

Four teenagers are sucked into a magical video game, and the only way they can escape is to work together to finish the game.

Main Leads: Dwayne Johnson, Kevin Hart, Karen Gillan, Jack Black | Year: 2017 | Genre: Action, Comedy, Adventure

The Conjuring (2013)

Paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren work to help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse.

Main Leads: Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson, Ron Livingston, Lili Taylor | Year: 2013 | Genre: Horror, Thriller
